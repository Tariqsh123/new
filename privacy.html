Privacy Policy

At British Book Publishers, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, billing details, and project-related information when you contact us, submit a form, place an order, or communicate with our team. We may also collect non-personal data such as browser type, IP address, and website usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to provide and improve our services, process payments, communicate with you, respond to inquiries, manage projects, and deliver customer support. We may also use your information to send updates, service-related notifications, or promotional content where legally permitted.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and improve functionality. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings; however, doing so may affect certain features of the website.

4. Data Protection and Security

British Book Publishers implements reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, or delivering services, provided they agree to keep the information confidential.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. British Book Publishers is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

7. Client Confidentiality

All client materials, manuscripts, designs, and project-related information are treated as confidential. We do not disclose or share client work without consent, except where required by law or agreed upon in writing.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also object to or restrict certain data processing activities. To exercise your rights, please contact us through our official communication channels.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If such data is discovered, it will be promptly removed.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

British Book Publishers reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of our website or services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes related to privacy matters shall fall under the jurisdiction of UK courts.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact British Book Publishers through our website or official support channels.
