Submission Guide:

We welcome manuscripts from all genres. Whether you're a new or published author, Gulf Publisher wants to hear from you. Our review process may take up to 48 hours, and we'll notify you once a decision is made.

To get your book considered for publishing, please send us details that include:

  1. A cover letter.
  2. A copy of your manuscript.
  3. Include any previous book details if you've been published before.
  4. Complete name, email ID and contact number.

To submit your manuscript, please fill the form with all details or email us at info@britishbookpublishers.com and we will get back to you.
